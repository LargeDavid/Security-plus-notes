# Day 5 Mini Lab – Phishing Attack

## Scenario
An employee enters credentials into a fake login page.

## What Happened
Credentials were stolen through phishing.

## What Attack Followed
Credential stuffing or unauthorized access attempts.

## Indicators
- Login attempts from multiple locations
- Unusual login times
- Failed login attempts

## Response Actions
- Reset credentials immediately
- Enable MFA
- Investigate logs
- Alert users

## Analyst Thinking
- Identify how credentials were stolen
- Check if other users are affected
- Determine scope of attack

## Key Takeaway
Phishing is often the starting point for larger attacks.
