# Day 2 Mini Lab – Suspicious Login Activity

## Scenario
Multiple failed login attempts are detected from different locations on a single account.

## Possible Causes
- Brute force attack
- Credential stuffing
- Stolen credentials

## Indicators
- Repeated login failures
- Different IP addresses
- Unusual login times

## What Should Be Done
- Lock or disable the account temporarily
- Investigate login logs
- Enable or enforce MFA
- Reset user credentials

## SOC Analyst Thinking
- Check logs for IP patterns
- Look for similar attempts across other accounts
- Determine if attack is widespread

## Key Takeaway
Unusual login behavior is often an early indicator of an attack.
